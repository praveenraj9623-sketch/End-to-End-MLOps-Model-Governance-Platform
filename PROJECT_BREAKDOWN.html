End-to-End MLOps & Model Governance Platform

Full project breakdown for cross-checking the implementation, dashboard behavior, workflows, and generated artifacts.

Project Idea

This is a local production-style MLOps governance demo for IBM HR Attrition. It trains attrition-risk models, selects a champion using business cost and PR-AUC, serves predictions through FastAPI, monitors drift, creates fairness diagnostics, writes privacy-aware audit logs, and displays everything in Streamlit.

FastAPI Streamlit MLflow local fallback DVC pipeline XGBoost champion Fairness report Drift simulation

What app.py Displays

TabPurposeMain Display
Executive SummaryPortfolio-level governance overview.AUC, PR-AUC, recall, precision, F1, business cost, drift status, calibration status, fairness availability, and risk distribution.
Model PerformanceCompare candidate models and thresholds.Candidate ranking, business-cost chart, threshold tradeoff chart, calibration curve, confusion matrix.
Model RegistryShow MLflow or local champion governance state.Registry rows, local fallback, generated model card JSON.
Drift MonitorSimulate production data shifts.Baseline, compensation shift, workload pressure, hiring mix, top drifted features, retraining flag.
ExplainabilityExplain one employee prediction.Employee selector, risk level, probability, recommendation, top local drivers, global feature importance.
Fairness & EthicsReview group-level model behavior.Gender, AgeGroup, Department, JobRole, MaritalStatus rates and review flags.
Audit TrailReview prediction logs.Hashed employee id, masked sensitive payload, probability, risk, threshold, action, and drivers.
